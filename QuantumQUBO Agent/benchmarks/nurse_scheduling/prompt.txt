Nurse Scheduling Assignment Problem

Given nurses, shifts, and assignment costs, assign exactly one nurse to each
shift. Each nurse can work at most one shift in these small benchmark cases.

Task:
Choose nurse-shift assignments.

Constraints:
- Every shift must be covered by exactly one nurse.
- A nurse may be assigned to at most one shift.

Objective:
Minimize the total assignment cost.

Goal:
Return the schedule and the minimum total cost.
