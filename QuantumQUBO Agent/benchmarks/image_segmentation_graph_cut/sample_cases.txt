IMAGE SEGMENTATION GRAPH-CUT TEST CASES

Source inspiration:
Supreeth Mysore Venkatesh, Antonio Macaluso, Marlon Nuske, Matthias Klusch,
and Andreas Dengel,
"Q-Seg: Quantum Annealing-Based Unsupervised Image Segmentation",
IEEE Computer Graphics and Applications, 2024.
DOI: https://doi.org/10.1109/MCG.2024.3455012

Shahrokh Heidari, Michael J. Dinneen, and Patrice J. Delmas,
"Quantum Annealing for Computer Vision minimization problems",
Future Generation Computer Systems 160, pp. 54-64, 2024.
DOI: https://doi.org/10.1016/j.future.2024.05.037

Benchmark simplification:
These are small binary-label graph-cut instances with precomputed energies.


========================================================================
TEST CASE 1: Three-pixel line

Pixels: P0, P1, P2
Labels: B, F

Candidate segmentations:
- S0: B, B, B with energy 3
- S1: F, F, F with energy 2
- S2: B, F, F with energy 1
- S3: B, B, F with energy 4

Ground truth:
- Minimum segmentation energy: 1
- Number of optimal segmentations: 1
- Selected segmentation:
  - S2
