TEST CASE 1: Two facilities, three unit-demand customers

Facilities:
- F0: opening cost 3, capacity 2
- F1: opening cost 2, capacity 2

Customers:
- C0: demand 1
- C1: demand 1
- C2: demand 1

Assignment costs:
- C0: F0=1, F1=4
- C1: F0=2, F1=1
- C2: F0=3, F1=1

Ground truth:
- Minimum total cost: 8
- Open facilities:
  - F0
  - F1
- Assignments:
  - C0 -> F0
  - C1 -> F1
  - C2 -> F1
