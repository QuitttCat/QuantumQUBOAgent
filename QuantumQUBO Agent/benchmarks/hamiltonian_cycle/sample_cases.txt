HAMILTONIAN CYCLE TEST CASES

Source inspiration:
Andrew Lucas,
"Ising formulations of many NP problems",
Frontiers in Physics 2, Article 5, 2014.
DOI: https://doi.org/10.3389/fphy.2014.00005

Benchmark simplification:
These cases list small graphs and candidate cycles. The objective is the number
of missing graph edges in the proposed cycle.
