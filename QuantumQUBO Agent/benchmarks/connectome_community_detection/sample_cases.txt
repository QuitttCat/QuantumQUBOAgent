BRAIN CONNECTOME COMMUNITY DETECTION TEST CASES

Source inspiration:
Marcin Wierzbinski, Joan Falco-Roget, and Alessandro Crimi,
"Community detection in brain connectomes with hybrid quantum computing",
Scientific Reports 13, Article 3446, 2023.
DOI: https://doi.org/10.1038/s41598-023-30579-y

Benchmark simplification:
The paper studies community detection in brain connectomes using D-Wave hybrid
quantum computing and modularity optimization. These benchmark cases use tiny
weighted connectome graphs with precomputed modularity-like scores for candidate
partitions.


========================================================================
TEST CASE 1: Two hemispheric modules with one weak bridge

Brain regions:
- L_Motor
- L_Visual
- R_Motor
- R_Visual

Weighted connectivity:
- L_Motor, L_Visual: 5
- R_Motor, R_Visual: 5
- L_Motor, R_Motor: 1
- L_Visual, R_Visual: 1
- L_Motor, R_Visual: 0
- L_Visual, R_Motor: 0

Candidate community partitions:
- P0:
  - Community C0: L_Motor, L_Visual
  - Community C1: R_Motor, R_Visual
  - modularity-like score: 10
- P1:
  - Community C0: L_Motor, R_Motor
  - Community C1: L_Visual, R_Visual
  - modularity-like score: 2
- P2:
  - Community C0: L_Motor, L_Visual, R_Motor, R_Visual
  - modularity-like score: 0

Ground truth:
- Maximum community score: 10
- QUBO minimization objective value: -10
- Number of optimal partitions: 1
- Selected partition:
  - P0

TEST CASE 2: Three functional systems in a small connectome

Brain regions:
- Visual_A
- Visual_B
- Motor_A
- Motor_B
- Default_A
- Default_B

Weighted connectivity:
- Visual_A, Visual_B: 6
- Motor_A, Motor_B: 5
- Default_A, Default_B: 6
- Visual_A, Motor_A: 1
- Visual_B, Motor_B: 1
- Motor_A, Default_A: 1
- Motor_B, Default_B: 1
- Visual_A, Default_A: 0
- Visual_B, Default_B: 0

Candidate community partitions:
- P0:
  - Community C0: Visual_A, Visual_B
  - Community C1: Motor_A, Motor_B
  - Community C2: Default_A, Default_B
  - modularity-like score: 17
- P1:
  - Community C0: Visual_A, Visual_B, Motor_A, Motor_B
  - Community C1: Default_A, Default_B
  - modularity-like score: 13
- P2:
  - Community C0: Visual_A, Motor_A, Default_A
  - Community C1: Visual_B, Motor_B, Default_B
  - modularity-like score: 4

Ground truth:
- Maximum community score: 17
- QUBO minimization objective value: -17
- Number of optimal partitions: 1
- Selected partition:
  - P0
