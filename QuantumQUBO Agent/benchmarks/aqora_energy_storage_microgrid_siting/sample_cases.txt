Benchmark simplification:
The Aqora/DOE challenge asks participants to investigate quantum formulations,
including QUBO or variational optimization frameworks, for strategic siting and
sizing of energy storage and microgrids under resilience and planning
constraints. These cases reduce the setting to selecting fixed-count candidate
sites with resilience benefits, investment costs, and overlap penalties.


========================================================================
TEST CASE 1: Choose two resilient infrastructure sites

Candidate sites:
- S0_hospital_zone:
  - resilience benefit: 10
  - investment cost: 3
- S1_data_center_zone:
  - resilience benefit: 9
  - investment cost: 4
- S2_industrial_zone:
  - resilience benefit: 7
  - investment cost: 2
- S3_residential_zone:
  - resilience benefit: 6
  - investment cost: 1

Task:
- Choose exactly K = 2 sites.

Pairwise overlap penalties:
- S0_hospital_zone, S1_data_center_zone: 3
- S0_hospital_zone, S2_industrial_zone: 1
- S0_hospital_zone, S3_residential_zone: 0
- S1_data_center_zone, S2_industrial_zone: 1
- S1_data_center_zone, S3_residential_zone: 1
- S2_industrial_zone, S3_residential_zone: 2

Ground truth:
- Maximum siting score: 12
- QUBO minimization objective value: -12
- Number of optimal selections: 1
- Selected sites:
  - S0_hospital_zone
  - S3_residential_zone
- Score calculation:
  - net site scores: (10 - 3) + (6 - 1) = 12
  - overlap penalty: 0
  - total siting score: 12

TEST CASE 2: Three-site deployment with overlapping service areas

Candidate sites:
- S0_hospital_zone:
  - resilience benefit: 11
  - investment cost: 4
- S1_port_zone:
  - resilience benefit: 8
  - investment cost: 2
- S2_data_center_zone:
  - resilience benefit: 10
  - investment cost: 5
- S3_rural_microgrid:
  - resilience benefit: 7
  - investment cost: 1
- S4_industrial_zone:
  - resilience benefit: 6
  - investment cost: 2

Task:
- Choose exactly K = 3 sites.

Pairwise overlap penalties:
- S0_hospital_zone, S1_port_zone: 1
- S0_hospital_zone, S2_data_center_zone: 3
- S0_hospital_zone, S3_rural_microgrid: 0
- S0_hospital_zone, S4_industrial_zone: 1
- S1_port_zone, S2_data_center_zone: 1
- S1_port_zone, S3_rural_microgrid: 0
- S1_port_zone, S4_industrial_zone: 2
- S2_data_center_zone, S3_rural_microgrid: 0
- S2_data_center_zone, S4_industrial_zone: 1
- S3_rural_microgrid, S4_industrial_zone: 1

Ground truth:
- Maximum siting score: 18
- QUBO minimization objective value: -18
- Number of optimal selections: 1
- Selected sites:
  - S0_hospital_zone
  - S1_port_zone
  - S3_rural_microgrid
- Score calculation:
  - net site scores: (11 - 4) + (8 - 2) + (7 - 1) = 19
  - overlap penalties: 1 + 0 + 0 = 1
  - total siting score: 18
