Energy Storage and Microgrid Siting Problem

Given candidate sites for energy storage systems or microgrids, choose a subset
of sites for deployment. Each site has a resilience benefit, an investment cost,
and pairwise overlap penalties when two sites protect the same load region.

Task:
Choose exactly K infrastructure sites.

Objective:
Maximize:

  total siting score =
    sum of selected resilience benefit scores
    - sum of selected site costs
    - sum of pairwise overlap penalties.

Goal:
Return the selected sites and maximum total siting score.

QUBO convention:
Use one binary variable per candidate site. Linear terms encode benefit and
investment cost. Quadratic terms encode overlap penalties and an exact-K
selection penalty.
