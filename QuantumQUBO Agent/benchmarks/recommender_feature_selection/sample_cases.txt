RECOMMENDER FEATURE SELECTION TEST CASES

Source inspiration:
Riccardo Nembrini, Maurizio Ferrari Dacrema, and Paolo Cremonesi,
"Feature Selection for Recommender Systems with Quantum Computing",
Entropy 23(8), 970, 2021.
DOI: https://doi.org/10.3390/e23080970

Giovanni Pilato and Filippo Vella,
"A Survey on Quantum Computing for Recommendation Systems",
Information 14(1), 20, 2023.
DOI: https://doi.org/10.3390/info14010020

Benchmark simplification:
These are small recommender feature-selection instances using utility and
redundancy scores. The objective is utility minus redundancy.


========================================================================
TEST CASE 1: Choose two recommender features

Number of features: 4
K: 2

Utility scores:
- F0_user_history: 9
- F1_item_genre: 6
- F2_price_band: 4
- F3_popularity: 7

Pairwise redundancy penalties:
- F0_user_history, F1_item_genre: 2
- F0_user_history, F2_price_band: 1
- F0_user_history, F3_popularity: 5
- F1_item_genre, F2_price_band: 1
- F1_item_genre, F3_popularity: 2
- F2_price_band, F3_popularity: 1

Ground truth:
- Maximum score: 13
- QUBO minimization objective value: -13
- Number of optimal solutions: 1
- Selected features:
  - F0_user_history
  - F1_item_genre
