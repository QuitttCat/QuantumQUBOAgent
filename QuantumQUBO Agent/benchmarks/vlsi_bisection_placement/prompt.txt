VLSI Bisection Placement Problem

Given circuit modules and weighted net connections between modules, assign each
module to one of two placement regions. The two regions must contain equal
numbers of modules.

Task:
Partition modules into two equal halves.

Objective:
Minimize total cut net weight between the two regions.

Goal:
Return the region assignment and minimum cut weight.

QUBO convention:
Use one binary variable per module. A quadratic balance penalty enforces equal
region size, and quadratic edge costs penalize nets crossing the bisection.
