Hospital Staff Rescheduling Problem

Given uncovered hospital shifts and available staff members, assign one qualified
staff member to each uncovered shift. Each assignment has a cost that can encode
fatigue, travel distance, overtime, or preference penalties.

Task:
Choose staff-shift assignments.

Constraints:
- Every uncovered shift must be assigned exactly one staff member.
- A staff member may cover at most one uncovered shift in these small cases.
- A staff member can only cover shifts for which they are qualified.

Objective:
Minimize total assignment cost.

Goal:
Return the replacement schedule and the minimum total cost.

QUBO convention:
Use one binary variable for each feasible staff-shift assignment. Quadratic
penalties enforce one staff member per shift and at most one shift per staff
member.
