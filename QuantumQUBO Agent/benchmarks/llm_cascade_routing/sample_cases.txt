LLM CASCADE ROUTING TEST CASES

Source inspiration:
Amit Patole,
"Quantum-Enhanced LLM Cascade Routing: A QAOA Approach to Cost-Optimal Model
Selection in Multi-Agent Systems",
Preprints.org, 2026.
DOI: https://doi.org/10.20944/preprints202604.0413.v2

Related LLM/QUBO source:
Huixiang Zhang, Mahzabeen Emu, and Salimur Choudhury,
"LLM-QUBO: An End-to-End Framework for Automated QUBO Transformation from
Natural Language Problem Descriptions",
Proceedings of the AAAI Symposium Series 7(1), 2025.
DOI: https://doi.org/10.1609/aaaiss.v7i1.36913

Benchmark simplification:
The cascade-routing paper formalizes LLM model-tier routing as a QUBO and solves
it using QAOA. These cases use small request-model tables with costs and quality
scores. Infeasible assignments are excluded by the quality threshold.


========================================================================
TEST CASE 1: Three requests, cheap model when quality allows

Requests:
- R0_simple_classification:
  - minimum quality: 2
- R1_math_reasoning:
  - minimum quality: 4
- R2_summarization:
  - minimum quality: 3

Model tiers:
- M0_small
- M1_medium
- M2_large

Assignment table:
- R0_simple_classification:
  - M0_small: cost 1, quality 2
  - M1_medium: cost 3, quality 4
  - M2_large: cost 6, quality 5
- R1_math_reasoning:
  - M0_small: cost 1, quality 1
  - M1_medium: cost 3, quality 4
  - M2_large: cost 6, quality 5
- R2_summarization:
  - M0_small: cost 1, quality 2
  - M1_medium: cost 3, quality 3
  - M2_large: cost 6, quality 5

Ground truth:
- Minimum total routing cost: 7
- Number of optimal assignments: 1
- Assignments:
  - R0_simple_classification -> M0_small
  - R1_math_reasoning -> M1_medium
  - R2_summarization -> M1_medium
- Score calculation:
  - 1 + 3 + 3 = 7

TEST CASE 2: One hard request requires the large model

Requests:
- R0_translation:
  - minimum quality: 3
- R1_code_debugging:
  - minimum quality: 5
- R2_sentiment:
  - minimum quality: 2
- R3_long_context_qa:
  - minimum quality: 4

Model tiers:
- M0_small
- M1_medium
- M2_large

Assignment table:
- R0_translation:
  - M0_small: cost 1, quality 2
  - M1_medium: cost 3, quality 3
  - M2_large: cost 7, quality 5
- R1_code_debugging:
  - M0_small: cost 1, quality 1
  - M1_medium: cost 3, quality 4
  - M2_large: cost 7, quality 5
- R2_sentiment:
  - M0_small: cost 1, quality 2
  - M1_medium: cost 3, quality 4
  - M2_large: cost 7, quality 5
- R3_long_context_qa:
  - M0_small: cost 1, quality 1
  - M1_medium: cost 3, quality 4
  - M2_large: cost 7, quality 5

Ground truth:
- Minimum total routing cost: 14
- Number of optimal assignments: 1
- Assignments:
  - R0_translation -> M1_medium
  - R1_code_debugging -> M2_large
  - R2_sentiment -> M0_small
  - R3_long_context_qa -> M1_medium
- Score calculation:
  - 3 + 7 + 1 + 3 = 14
