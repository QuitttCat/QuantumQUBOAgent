HAMILTONIAN PATH TEST CASES

Source inspiration:
Andrew Lucas,
"Ising formulations of many NP problems",
Frontiers in Physics 2, Article 5, 2014.
DOI: https://doi.org/10.3389/fphy.2014.00005

Benchmark simplification:
These instances are kept at n=4 vertices so the natural vertex-position
encoding (x_{v,p} for v vertices, p positions) yields 16 binary variables,
allowing exact 2^n verification of the QUBO ground state.
