Benchmark simplification:
The IonQ/Kipu work decomposes a large S&P 500 cardinality-constrained portfolio
QUBO into hardware-executable trapped-ion subproblems, solves subproblems, and
recombines feasible global portfolios. These cases represent one small
hardware-sized cluster QUBO with integer return and risk scores.


========================================================================
TEST CASE 1: Four-asset cluster, choose two

Cluster assets:
- A0_tech: return score 9
- A1_cloud: return score 7
- A2_health: return score 6
- A3_utility: return score 4

Cardinality:
- K = 2

Pairwise risk/correlation penalties:
- A0_tech, A1_cloud: 5
- A0_tech, A2_health: 2
- A0_tech, A3_utility: 1
- A1_cloud, A2_health: 1
- A1_cloud, A3_utility: 1
- A2_health, A3_utility: 0

Ground truth:
- Maximum cluster portfolio score: 12
- QUBO minimization objective value: -12
- Number of optimal selections: 1
- Selected assets:
  - A0_tech
  - A3_utility
- Score calculation:
  - return: 9 + 4 = 13
  - risk penalty: 1
  - total score: 12
