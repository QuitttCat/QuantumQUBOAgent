Unit Commitment Toy Problem

Given generators, time periods, demand, fixed on-costs, and production costs,
choose which generators are on and how much each generator produces.

Task:
Meet demand in every time period.

Constraints:
- A generator can produce only when it is on.
- Production must not exceed generator capacity.
- Demand must be met exactly in these small cases.

Objective:
Minimize fixed commitment costs plus production costs.

Goal:
Return the unit schedule, dispatch, and minimum total cost.
