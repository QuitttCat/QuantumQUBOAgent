MUTUAL-INFORMATION SENSOR PLACEMENT TEST CASES

Source inspiration:
Yuta Nakano and Shigeyasu Uno,
"Quadratic Formulation of Mutual Information for Sensor Placement Optimization
using Ising and Quantum Annealing Machines",
arXiv:2407.14747, 2024.
DOI: https://doi.org/10.48550/arXiv.2407.14747

Benchmark simplification:
The paper formulates sensor placement by mutual information as a QUBO for Ising
and quantum annealing machines. These cases use small information scores and
pairwise redundancy penalties as a compact surrogate for mutual information.


========================================================================
TEST CASE 1: Four candidate sensor positions

Candidate sensors:
- P0_upstream: information score 8
- P1_midstream: information score 7
- P2_downstream: information score 6
- P3_control: information score 4

Task:
- Choose exactly K = 2 sensors.

Pairwise redundancy penalties:
- P0_upstream, P1_midstream: 4
- P0_upstream, P2_downstream: 1
- P0_upstream, P3_control: 0
- P1_midstream, P2_downstream: 2
- P1_midstream, P3_control: 1
- P2_downstream, P3_control: 1

Ground truth:
- Maximum information score: 13
- QUBO minimization objective value: -13
- Number of optimal selections: 1
- Selected sensors:
  - P0_upstream
  - P2_downstream
- Score calculation:
  - P0, P2: 8 + 6 - 1 = 13


========================================================================
TEST CASE 2: Five positions, choose three

Candidate sensors:
- P0_north: information score 7
- P1_south: information score 6
- P2_east: information score 6
- P3_west: information score 5
- P4_center: information score 8

Task:
- Choose exactly K = 3 sensors.

Pairwise redundancy penalties:
- P0_north, P1_south: 1
- P0_north, P2_east: 2
- P0_north, P3_west: 1
- P0_north, P4_center: 4
- P1_south, P2_east: 1
- P1_south, P3_west: 2
- P1_south, P4_center: 3
- P2_east, P3_west: 1
- P2_east, P4_center: 2
- P3_west, P4_center: 2

Ground truth:
- Maximum information score: 15
- QUBO minimization objective value: -15
- Number of optimal selections: 1
- Selected sensors:
  - P0_north
  - P1_south
  - P2_east
- Score calculation:
  - information: 7 + 6 + 6 = 19
  - redundancy: 1 + 2 + 1 = 4
  - total score: 15
