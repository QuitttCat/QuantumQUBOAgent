Molecular Conformation Selection Problem

Given a molecule decomposed into rotatable torsions, choose one discrete
rotamer state for each torsion. Each rotamer has a local energy, and pairs of
rotamers have interaction penalties or rewards.

Task:
Choose exactly one rotamer for each torsion.

Objective:
Minimize:

  total energy =
    sum of selected rotamer local energies
    + sum of selected pairwise interaction terms.

Goal:
Return the selected rotamers and the minimum total energy.
