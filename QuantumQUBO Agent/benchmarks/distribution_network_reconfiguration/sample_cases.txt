DISTRIBUTION NETWORK RECONFIGURATION TEST CASES

Source inspiration:
Filipe F. C. Silva, Pedro M. S. Carvalho, and Luis A. F. M. Ferreira,
"A quantum computing approach for minimum loss problems in electrical
distribution networks",
Scientific Reports 13, Article 10777, 2023.
DOI: https://doi.org/10.1038/s41598-023-37293-9

Benchmark simplification:
The full problem uses QUBO for network loss minimization in distribution grids.
These cases provide candidate radial configurations with precomputed costs.
