FINANCIAL FRAUD FEATURE SELECTION TEST CASES

Source inspiration:
Sutapa Samanta, Dagen Wang, Todd Hodges, and Andras Ferenczi,
"Parametric Quantum Feature Selection Methods for Fraud and Default Detection",
Proceedings of the AAAI Symposium Series 7(1), pp. 390-397, 2025.
DOI: https://doi.org/10.1609/aaaiss.v7i1.36910

Benchmark simplification:
These cases use small predictive-value and redundancy tables for financial risk
feature selection. The QUBO structure is cardinality-constrained feature
selection.
