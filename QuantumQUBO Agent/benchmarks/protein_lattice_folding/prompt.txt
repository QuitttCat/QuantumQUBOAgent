HP Lattice Protein Conformation Selection Problem

Given a short hydrophobic-polar protein sequence and a curated list of candidate
lattice conformations, choose the conformation with the lowest contact energy.

Each candidate conformation lists the nonconsecutive hydrophobic-hydrophobic
contacts it creates. Each such contact contributes -1 energy.

Task:
Choose exactly one candidate conformation.

Objective:
Minimize total contact energy.

Goal:
Return the selected conformation and its minimum energy.
