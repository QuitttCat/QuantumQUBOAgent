TOP EIGENCENTRALITY NODE SELECTION TEST CASES

Source inspiration:
Prosper D. Akrobotu, Tamsin E. James, Christian F. A. Negre, and Susan M.
Mniszewski,
"A QUBO formulation for top-tau eigencentrality nodes",
PLOS ONE 17(7), e0271292, 2022.
DOI: https://doi.org/10.1371/journal.pone.0271292

Benchmark simplification:
The paper derives QUBO structure from eigencentrality. These small tests provide
centrality scores directly and enforce selecting exactly tau nodes.
