Energy Storage Scheduling Problem

Given a battery and electricity prices over time, choose one action at each time
step: charge, idle, or discharge. Battery state of charge must remain within
capacity bounds.

Task:
Choose a feasible action sequence.

Objective:
Minimize net energy cost:

  cost =
    cost of charging at the current price
    - revenue from discharging at the current price.

Goal:
Return the action sequence and minimum net cost.
