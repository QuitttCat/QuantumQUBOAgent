Multi-Vehicle Delivery Assignment Problem

Given delivery jobs and vehicles, choose one route option for each vehicle so
that customer jobs are covered exactly once. Each route has a delivery cost and
covers a subset of jobs.

Task:
Select compatible vehicle routes.

Constraints:
- Each customer job must be covered exactly once.
- Each vehicle may use at most one route.

Objective:
Minimize total delivery cost.

Goal:
Return the selected vehicle routes and minimum total cost.

QUBO convention:
Use one binary variable per candidate route. Quadratic penalties enforce exact
customer coverage and prevent selecting two routes for the same vehicle.
