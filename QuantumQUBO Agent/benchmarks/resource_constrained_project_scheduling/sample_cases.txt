RESOURCE-CONSTRAINED PROJECT SCHEDULING TEST CASES

Source inspiration:
Luis Fernando Perez Armas, Stefan Creemers, and Samuel Deleplanque,
"Solving the resource constrained project scheduling problem with quantum
annealing",
Scientific Reports 14, Article 16784, 2024.
DOI: https://doi.org/10.1038/s41598-024-67168-6

Benchmark simplification:
These cases use one renewable resource and integer time. Binary start-time
variables are the natural QUBO encoding.


========================================================================
TEST CASE 1: Two parallel activities under resource capacity

Resource capacity: 3

Activities:
- A0: duration 2, demand 2, predecessors none
- A1: duration 2, demand 2, predecessors none
- A2: duration 1, demand 1, predecessors A0 and A1

Ground truth:
- Minimum makespan: 5
- One optimal schedule:
  - A0 starts at 0
  - A1 starts at 2
  - A2 starts at 4


========================================================================
TEST CASE 2: Compatible overlap before final task

Resource capacity: 4

Activities:
- A0: duration 2, demand 2, predecessors none
- A1: duration 3, demand 2, predecessors none
- A2: duration 1, demand 2, predecessors A0
- A3: duration 1, demand 1, predecessors A1 and A2

Ground truth:
- Minimum makespan: 4
- One optimal schedule:
  - A0 starts at 0
  - A1 starts at 0
  - A2 starts at 2
  - A3 starts at 3
