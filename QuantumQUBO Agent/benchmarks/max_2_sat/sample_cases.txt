TEST CASE 1: Three variables, four weighted clauses

Variables: x0, x1, x2

Clauses:
- C0: x0 OR x1, weight 3
- C1: NOT x0 OR x2, weight 2
- C2: NOT x1 OR NOT x2, weight 4
- C3: x1 OR x2, weight 1

Ground truth:
- Maximum satisfied weight: 10
- Number of optimal assignments: 2
- Optimal assignments:
  - x0 = false, x1 = true, x2 = false
  - x0 = true, x1 = false, x2 = true
