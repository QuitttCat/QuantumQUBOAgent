Densest K-Subgraph Problem

Given an undirected weighted graph, choose exactly K vertices so that the total
weight of edges induced by the chosen vertices is maximized.

Task:
Choose exactly K vertices.

Objective:
Maximize the induced edge weight.

Goal:
Return the selected vertices and maximum induced edge weight.

QUBO convention:
Use one binary variable per vertex with a quadratic cardinality penalty. Edge
weights become quadratic rewards between selected vertex variables.
