DENSEST K-SUBGRAPH TEST CASES

Source inspiration:
Cristian S. Calude, Michael J. Dinneen, and Richard Hua,
"Quantum solutions for densest k-subgraph problems",
Journal of Membrane Computing 2, pp. 130-139, 2020.
DOI: https://doi.org/10.1007/s41965-019-00030-1

Benchmark simplification:
These are small weighted graph instances. The score is the total weight of
selected-induced edges.


========================================================================
TEST CASE 1: Four vertices, choose three

K: 3

Edges:
- 0, 1, weight 4
- 0, 2, weight 3
- 0, 3, weight 1
- 1, 2, weight 5
- 1, 3, weight 1
- 2, 3, weight 2

Ground truth:
- Maximum induced edge weight: 12
- QUBO minimization objective value: -12
- Number of optimal solutions: 1
- Selected vertices:
  - 0
  - 1
  - 2
