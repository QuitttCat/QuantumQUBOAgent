CYBER RISK SCORING TEST CASES

Source inspiration:
"Cyber Risk Scoring with QUBO: A Quantum and Hybrid Benchmark Study",
arXiv:2512.18305, 2025.
DOI: https://doi.org/10.48550/arXiv.2512.18305

Benchmark simplification:
The source formulates cyber risk assessment in complex IT infrastructures as a
QUBO-compatible benchmark and compares classical, quantum, and hybrid workflows.
These cases reduce the setting to selecting high-risk infrastructure assets
while penalizing redundant risk propagation coverage.


========================================================================
TEST CASE 1: Four assets, choose two remediation priorities

Assets:
- A0_public_gateway: risk score 9
- A1_identity_server: risk score 8
- A2_database: risk score 7
- A3_monitoring_node: risk score 4

Task:
- Choose exactly K = 2 assets.

Pairwise overlap penalties:
- A0_public_gateway, A1_identity_server: 4
- A0_public_gateway, A2_database: 1
- A0_public_gateway, A3_monitoring_node: 1
- A1_identity_server, A2_database: 2
- A1_identity_server, A3_monitoring_node: 1
- A2_database, A3_monitoring_node: 0

Ground truth:
- Maximum risk-prioritization score: 15
- QUBO minimization objective value: -15
- Number of optimal selections: 1
- Selected assets:
  - A0_public_gateway
  - A2_database
- Score calculation:
  - risk score: 9 + 7 = 16
  - overlap penalty: 1
  - total score: 15

TEST CASE 2: Five assets with redundant attack paths

Assets:
- A0_vpn: risk score 8
- A1_domain_controller: risk score 10
- A2_file_server: risk score 6
- A3_backup_server: risk score 5
- A4_cloud_connector: risk score 7

Task:
- Choose exactly K = 3 assets.

Pairwise overlap penalties:
- A0_vpn, A1_domain_controller: 3
- A0_vpn, A2_file_server: 1
- A0_vpn, A3_backup_server: 0
- A0_vpn, A4_cloud_connector: 2
- A1_domain_controller, A2_file_server: 4
- A1_domain_controller, A3_backup_server: 2
- A1_domain_controller, A4_cloud_connector: 1
- A2_file_server, A3_backup_server: 3
- A2_file_server, A4_cloud_connector: 1
- A3_backup_server, A4_cloud_connector: 1

Ground truth:
- Maximum risk-prioritization score: 19
- QUBO minimization objective value: -19
- Number of optimal selections: 1
- Selected assets:
  - A0_vpn
  - A1_domain_controller
  - A4_cloud_connector
- Score calculation:
  - risk score: 8 + 10 + 7 = 25
  - overlap penalty: 3 + 2 + 1 = 6
  - total score: 19
