Cyber Risk Scoring Node Selection Problem

Given an infrastructure graph with assets and dependencies, choose a set of
assets for priority remediation or inspection. Each asset has a risk score, and
pairs of assets have propagation-overlap penalties when they represent the same
risk pathway.

Task:
Choose exactly K assets.

Objective:
Maximize:

  total risk-prioritization score =
    sum of selected asset risk scores
    - sum of selected pairwise overlap penalties.

Goal:
Return the selected assets and maximum risk-prioritization score.

QUBO convention:
Use one binary variable per asset. A quadratic penalty enforces exactly K
selected assets, and pairwise terms encode overlapping cyber-risk pathways.
