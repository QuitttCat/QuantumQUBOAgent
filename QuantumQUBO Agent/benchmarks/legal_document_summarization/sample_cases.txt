Benchmark simplification:
These cases treat legal summarization as cardinality-constrained extractive
sentence selection. Importance scores represent legal salience, such as facts,
issues, reasoning, precedent, or holding. Redundancy penalties discourage
selecting sentences that repeat the same legal role.


========================================================================
TEST CASE 1: Small case brief, choose two sentences

Number of candidate sentences: 4
K: 2

Sentence importance scores:
- S0_facts: 7
- S1_issue: 8
- S2_reasoning: 9
- S3_holding: 6

Pairwise redundancy penalties:
- S0_facts, S1_issue: 1
- S0_facts, S2_reasoning: 1
- S0_facts, S3_holding: 0
- S1_issue, S2_reasoning: 3
- S1_issue, S3_holding: 1
- S2_reasoning, S3_holding: 2

Ground truth:
- Maximum summary score: 15
- QUBO minimization objective value: -15
- Number of optimal summaries: 1
- Selected sentences:
  - S0_facts
  - S2_reasoning
- Score calculation:
  - S0_facts + S2_reasoning: 7 + 9 - 1 = 15

TEST CASE 2: Appeal judgment, choose three roles

Number of candidate sentences: 5
K: 3

Sentence importance scores:
- S0_background: 5
- S1_statute: 7
- S2_precedent: 8
- S3_reasoning: 9
- S4_disposition: 6

Pairwise redundancy penalties:
- S0_background, S1_statute: 0
- S0_background, S2_precedent: 1
- S0_background, S3_reasoning: 1
- S0_background, S4_disposition: 0
- S1_statute, S2_precedent: 2
- S1_statute, S3_reasoning: 1
- S1_statute, S4_disposition: 1
- S2_precedent, S3_reasoning: 3
- S2_precedent, S4_disposition: 1
- S3_reasoning, S4_disposition: 1

Ground truth:
- Maximum summary score: 19
- QUBO minimization objective value: -19
- Number of optimal summaries: 2
- Selected sentence sets:
  - S0_background, S1_statute, S3_reasoning
  - S1_statute, S3_reasoning, S4_disposition
- Score calculation:
  - S0, S1, S3: 5 + 7 + 9 - 0 - 1 - 1 = 19
  - S1, S3, S4: 7 + 9 + 6 - 1 - 1 - 1 = 19
