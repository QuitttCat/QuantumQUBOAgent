Legal Document Extractive Summarization Problem

Given a legal document split into candidate sentences, choose exactly K sentences
for an extractive summary. Each sentence has an importance score, and pairs of
sentences can have redundancy penalties if they cover the same legal point.

Task:
Choose exactly K sentences.

Objective:
Maximize:

  total summary score =
    sum of selected sentence importance scores
    - sum of redundancy penalties between selected sentence pairs.

Goal:
Return the selected sentence IDs and maximum summary score.

QUBO convention:
Use one binary variable per candidate sentence. The exactly-K summary length
constraint is encoded as a quadratic penalty. If using minimization, minimize the
negative summary score plus the length penalty.
