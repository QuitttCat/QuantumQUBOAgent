SOCIAL INFLUENCE MAXIMIZATION TEST CASES

Source inspiration:
Thang N. Dinh, An Nguyen, Uyen Nguyen, and Giang Nguyen,
"Quantum Social Computing Approaches for Influence Maximization",
IEEE GLOBECOM 2022, pp. 5832-5837.
DOI: https://doi.org/10.1109/GLOBECOM48099.2022.10000698

Related influence maximization source:
Qingye Jiang, Guojie Song, Cong Gao, Yu Wang, Wenjun Si, and Kunqing Xie,
"Simulated Annealing Based Influence Maximization in Social Networks",
Proceedings of the AAAI Conference on Artificial Intelligence 25(1), 2011.
DOI: https://doi.org/10.1609/aaai.v25i1.7838

Benchmark simplification:
The quantum social computing paper converts influence maximization into a
Max-Cover instance and then into QUBO formulations for quantum annealers. These
cases provide small seed-reach sets and ask for maximum covered users.


========================================================================
TEST CASE 1: Four candidate influencers, choose two

Users to influence: U0, U1, U2, U3, U4

Candidate seed reach sets:
- S0: reaches U0, U1, U2
- S1: reaches U2, U3
- S2: reaches U3, U4
- S3: reaches U0, U4

Task:
- Choose exactly K = 2 seeds.

Ground truth:
- Maximum influenced users: 5
- QUBO minimization objective value: -5
- Number of optimal seed sets: 1
- Selected seeds:
  - S0
  - S2
- Coverage:
  - S0 union S2 = U0, U1, U2, U3, U4


========================================================================
TEST CASE 2: Overlapping communities

Users to influence: U0, U1, U2, U3, U4, U5

Candidate seed reach sets:
- S0: reaches U0, U1, U2
- S1: reaches U1, U2, U3
- S2: reaches U3, U4
- S3: reaches U4, U5
- S4: reaches U0, U5

Task:
- Choose exactly K = 2 seeds.

Ground truth:
- Maximum influenced users: 5
- QUBO minimization objective value: -5
- Number of optimal seed sets: 4
- Optimal seed sets:
  - S0, S3
  - S1, S3
  - S1, S4
  - S2, S4
