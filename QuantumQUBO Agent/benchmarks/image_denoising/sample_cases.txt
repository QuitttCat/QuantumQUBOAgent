BINARY IMAGE DENOISING TEST CASES

Source inspiration:
Phillip Kerger and Ryoji Miyazaki,
"Quantum image denoising: a framework via Boltzmann machines, QUBO, and
quantum annealing",
Frontiers in Computer Science 5, 1281100, 2023.
DOI: https://doi.org/10.3389/fcomp.2023.1281100

Benchmark simplification:
These cases list candidate clean binary images and their precomputed QUBO-style
denoising energies.


========================================================================
TEST CASE 1: Two-pixel denoising

Observed noisy pixels:
- P0: 1
- P1: 0

Candidate clean images:
- C00: P0=0, P1=0, energy 1
- C01: P0=0, P1=1, energy 3
- C10: P0=1, P1=0, energy 2
- C11: P0=1, P1=1, energy 1

Ground truth:
- Minimum denoising energy: 1
- Number of optimal clean images: 2
- Selected clean images:
  - C00
  - C11


========================================================================
TEST CASE 2: Four-pixel block with one noisy corner

Observed noisy image:
- P0=1, P1=1
- P2=1, P3=0

Candidate clean images:
- C_all0: 0,0,0,0 with energy 3
- C_all1: 1,1,1,1 with energy 1
- C_split_rows: 1,1,0,0 with energy 4
- C_observed: 1,1,1,0 with energy 2

Ground truth:
- Minimum denoising energy: 1
- Number of optimal clean images: 1
- Selected clean image:
  - C_all1
