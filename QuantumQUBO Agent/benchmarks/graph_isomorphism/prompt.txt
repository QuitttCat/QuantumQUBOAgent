Graph Isomorphism Mapping Problem

Given two graphs G and H with the same number of vertices, choose a one-to-one
mapping from vertices of G to vertices of H.

Task:
Find a bijection that preserves adjacency.

Constraints:
- Each vertex of G maps to exactly one vertex of H.
- Each vertex of H is used exactly once.

Objective:
Minimize the number of adjacency mismatches under the mapping.

Goal:
Return the vertex mapping and the minimum number of mismatches. A value of 0
means the graphs are isomorphic.
