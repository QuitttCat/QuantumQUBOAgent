FACILITY LOCATION TEST CASES

Source inspiration:
Luis A. Moncayo-Martinez and Naihui He,
"Quantum optimisation for supply chain: QUBO formulations and QAOA solutions
for facility location and load balancing",
Results in Engineering 29, 108373, 2026.
DOI: https://doi.org/10.1016/j.rineng.2025.108373

M. Battaglino et al.,
"Quantum annealing for the two-level facility location problem",
Future Generation Computer Systems 174, 107961, 2026.
DOI: https://doi.org/10.1016/j.future.2025.107961

Benchmark simplification:
These cases use one-level uncapacitated facility location with small integer
costs.


========================================================================
TEST CASE 1: Two candidate depots, three customers

Facilities:
- F0: opening cost 4
- F1: opening cost 3

Assignment costs:
- Customer C0: F0=1, F1=3
- Customer C1: F0=2, F1=1
- Customer C2: F0=4, F1=1

Ground truth:
- Minimum total cost: 8
- Number of optimal solutions: 1
- Open facilities:
  - F1
- Assignments:
  - C0 -> F1
  - C1 -> F1
  - C2 -> F1


========================================================================
TEST CASE 2: Opening both facilities is best

Facilities:
- F0: opening cost 3
- F1: opening cost 3

Assignment costs:
- Customer C0: F0=1, F1=5
- Customer C1: F0=1, F1=4
- Customer C2: F0=5, F1=1
- Customer C3: F0=4, F1=1

Ground truth:
- Minimum total cost: 10
- Number of optimal solutions: 1
- Open facilities:
  - F0
  - F1
- Assignments:
  - C0 -> F0
  - C1 -> F0
  - C2 -> F1
  - C3 -> F1
